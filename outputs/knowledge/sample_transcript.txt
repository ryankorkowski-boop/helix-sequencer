To do model groups in xLights, click Layout, create a model group called Arches_All, and include all arch models.
Right click the sequence grid and drag the Chase effect onto the model group row.
If this happens and one arch runs backward, reverse that arch direction in model orientation.
Common mistake: applying both effects to individual arches instead of the group.
For WLED controllers, map universes carefully and verify output on the preview before export.
