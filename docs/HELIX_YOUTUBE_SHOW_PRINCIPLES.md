# Removed: Source-Specific Show Principles

Status: Removed from active guidance.

This file previously used source-specific framing around online show quality. Even though the guidance was generalized and included non-copying rules, the title and framing are audit-noisy. Helix should not retain guidance that can be read as derived from YouTube shows, vendor sequences, creator styles, tutorial recipes, or paid displays.

Use source-agnostic guidance instead:

- `docs/helix_master_rulebook.md`
- `docs/legal_learning_policy.md`

Future show-direction rules must be written as original, generalized, testable engineering criteria based on lawful sources, Helix-generated experiments, and user-authored/user-confirmed material only.
